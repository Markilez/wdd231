Site Name

Name: AutoCare Hub

Description: The name "AutoCare Hub" reflects the garage's focus on providing comprehensive automotive care and maintenance services. It emphasizes reliability, expertise, and a central place for all car-related needs.

Optional Domain: autocarehub.com

Site Purpose

The AutoCare Hub website will serve as an informational and interactive platform for customers seeking automotive repair and maintenance services. It will provide:

  • Detailed descriptions of services (e.g., oil changes, tire rotations, brake repairs).
  • Information about the garage's history, mission, and team.
  • Customer testimonials to build trust.
  • A contact form for inquiries and appointment scheduling.
  • A blog with car maintenance tips, industry news, and promotions.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: "What types of car maintenance services does AutoCare Hub offer, and how can I schedule an appointment?"
    Answer: The "Services" page will list all available services, and the "Contact Us" page will include a booking form.
  2. Scenario 2: "How can I trust AutoCare Hub with my car repairs?"
    Answer: The "Testimonials" page will feature customer reviews, and the "About Us" page will highlight the garage's experience and certifications.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Navy Blue (#003366) – Used for headings, navigation bar, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) – Used for background and paragraph text.
  • Accent Color: Orange (#FF6600) – Used for buttons, links, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Roboto (Sans-serif) – Used for headings and navigation.
  • Secondary Font: Open Sans (Sans-serif) – Used for body text and paragraphs.
  • Accent Font: Lato (Sans-serif) – Used for buttons and special sections.

Wireframe

Mobile View:

  1. Header with logo and navigation menu (collapsible).
  2. Hero section with a welcoming message and call-to-action button.
  3. Brief overview of services with icons.
  4. Footer with contact information and social media links.

Desktop View:

  1. Header with logo and horizontal navigation menu.
  2. Hero section with a large image, welcoming message, and call-to-action button.
  3. Grid layout for services with icons and short descriptions.
  4. Footer with contact form, phone numbers, and social media links.
